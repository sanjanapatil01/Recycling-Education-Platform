How to Recycle - Step by Step

Learn how to recycle properly and protect the environment!

Step 1: Sort Your Waste

Start by sorting your waste into categories like paper, plastic, glass, and metal. Keep recyclables separate from regular trash.

Step 2: Clean and Rinse Items

Ensure that the recyclables are clean. Rinse containers to remove food and liquids, as dirty items can contaminate the recycling stream.

Step 3: Check Local Guidelines

Check your local recycling program’s guidelines. Some areas have specific rules about what can and can’t be recycled.

Step 4: Place Items in the Bin

Place the clean, sorted recyclables into the appropriate bin. Make sure the bin is not overfilled, as that can cause contamination.

Step 5: Drop Off or Pick Up

Once your recyclables are sorted and placed in the bin, drop them off at a recycling center or wait for your curbside pickup.
